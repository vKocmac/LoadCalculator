HVAC Load Calculator

Cooling/heating load estimation tool with guided psychrometrics for comfort and light-industrial applications.

Typical Use
1) Fill inputs from top to bottom.
2) Calculate load and inspect split (Qs/Ql).
3) Open psychrometrics for SA + coil validation.

Τύποι που χρησιμοποιούνται
Q_trans = U * A * ΔT
Q_sensible_air = m_dot * cp * ΔT
Q_latent_air = m_dot * (h_out - h_in) - Q_sensible
SHR = Qs / (Qs + Ql)
Q_coil_total = m_dot * (h_mix - h_supply)
