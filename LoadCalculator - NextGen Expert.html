Acronyms & Key Terms
RA

Return air from the room.

OA

Outdoor fresh air.

SA

Supply air to room.

Tsa

Supply air dry-bulb temperature.

w

Humidity ratio (kg moisture per kg dry air).

h

Moist-air enthalpy (kJ/kg).

Qs / Ql

Sensible / latent load components.

SHR

Qs/(Qs+Ql).

ADP

Coil apparatus dew point.

BF

Bypass factor.
